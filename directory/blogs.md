# Blogs & Newsletters Directory

> Active blogs and newsletters covering Obsidian, PKM, and tools for thought. The README's [Resources section](../README.md#blogs--newsletters) lists curated picks; this is the broader index.
>
> Last reviewed: 2026-04-25.

## Newsletters

- [**PKM Weekly**](https://www.pkmweekly.com) — Weekly roundup of PKM tools, articles, and Obsidian ecosystem news.
- [**DeveloPassion's Newsletter (Sébastien Dubois)**](https://www.dsebastien.net) — PKM expertise, Obsidian Starter Kit, knowledge worker strategies.
- [**Practical PKM**](https://practicalpkm.com) — Tips and workflows to improve Obsidian-based productivity and creativity.
- [**Manuscriptions (Eleanor Konik)**](https://www.eleanorkonik.com) — Worldbuilding, history, weird-animal biology, and how she uses Obsidian to write fiction.

## Blogs

- [**Official Obsidian Blog**](https://obsidian.md/blog) — Releases, ecosystem updates, and Obsidian team announcements.
- [**Obsibrain**](https://obsibrain.com/blog) — Plugin guides for power users.
- [**Effortless Academic**](https://effortlessacademic.com) — Academic workflows with Zotero integration and research pipelines.
- [**Face Dragons**](https://facedragons.com/foss) — FOSS-focused Obsidian guides and reviews.
- [**Bryan Jenks**](https://www.bryanjenks.dev/blog) — Zettelkasten, Obsidian + Anki workflows, ADHD-aware PKM.
- [**Curtis McHale**](https://curtismchale.ca) — Productivity, writing workflows, book notes.
- [**Linking Your Thinking (Nick Milo)**](https://www.linkingyourthinking.com) — LYT framework, MOCs, Ideaverse starter vault, PKM courses.
- [**The Obsidian Observer (Medium)**](https://medium.com/obsidian-observer) — Publication on Obsidian ideas, plugins, and methodology.

## Adjacent (Tools for Thought / PKM Theory)

- [**Maggie Appleton**](https://maggieappleton.com) — Digital gardens, illustrated essays on PKM and tools for thought.
- [**Andy Matuschak**](https://andymatuschak.org) — Evergreen notes methodology, spaced repetition, knowledge work.

## Discovery & Stats

- [**Obsidian Stats**](https://www.obsidianstats.com) — Real-time plugin rankings, trending charts, download metrics.
- [**Obsidian Mate**](https://obsidianmate.com) — Plugin browsing and theme previews.
