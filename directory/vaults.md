# Starter Vaults & Templates Directory

> Public starter vaults, template systems, and reference vaults you can browse, fork, or learn from.
>
> Last reviewed: 2026-04-25.

## Starter Vaults

- [**Ideaverse for Obsidian (LYT)**](https://www.linkingyourthinking.com) — Nick Milo's flagship starter vault and template system, built around the LYT framework and Maps of Content.
- [**Obsidian Sample Vault**](https://github.com/obsidianmd/obsidian-sample-plugin) — Official sample vault structure included with the plugin development template.

## Curated Hubs

- [**Obsidian Hub**](https://publish.obsidian.md/hub) — Browseable community-curated vault published with Obsidian Publish. Contains resources, talks, templates, and showcases. The vault itself can be downloaded from [obsidian-community/obsidian-hub](https://github.com/obsidian-community/obsidian-hub).

## Life OS & Journaling Vaults

Full-vault systems for life management, daily journaling, and periodic review.

- [**LifeOS Example Vault**](https://github.com/quanru/obsidian-example-lifeos) — 2024 Obsidian Gems award (3rd place). PARA + periodic notes. Built around weekly reviews.
- [**Life Ops Starter**](https://github.com/replete/life-ops-starter-obsidian-vault) — Daily templates, Zettelkasten, QuickAdd, and Day Planner wired together.
- [**Obsidian Workflow Template**](https://github.com/mathisgauthey/obsidian-workflow-template) — Tasks, projects, journaling, media tracking, and read-it-later in one vault.

## Dashboard Templates

Homepage and mission-control layouts for the vault.

- [**Obsidian Homepage (Rainbell)**](https://github.com/Rainbell129/Obsidian-Homepage) — Full-featured dashboard with MOC navigation, project tracking, reading list, and daily stats.
- [**Obsidian Homepage (faroukx)**](https://github.com/faroukx/obsidian-homepage) — Minimal aesthetic homepage with customizable widgets.

## Finance Templates

Personal finance and budgeting built on Dataview and frontmatter.

- [**Personal Finance Vault**](https://github.com/LBerts/personal-finance) — Monthly summary cards, balance charts, and spending-by-category via Dataview.
- [**Expensica**](https://github.com/dhruvir-zala/obsidian-expensica) — Daily expense tracker using frontmatter properties and Dataview dashboards.

## Health & Habit Templates

- [**Habit + Mood Tracker**](https://github.com/corinnekaryiu/obsidian-habit-mood-tracker) — Habit and mood tracking via daily notes with heatmap and chart views.

## Role-Specific Starter Vaults

Opinionated setups for specific roles — fork and adapt.

- [**Student Starter Vault**](https://github.com/shawngraham/obsidian-student-starter-vault) — Academic research vault with citation workflow, reading notes, and essay drafting.
- [**Novel Starter Vault**](https://github.com/rrbaker/obsidian-novel-starter-vault) — Long-form writing vault for novels — character sheets, chapter tracking, world-building.
- [**History Research Vault**](https://github.com/erazlogo/obsidian-history-vault) — Academic history research with Zotero integration and structured source management.
- [**PARA Starter**](https://github.com/voidashi/obsidian-vault-template) — Clean PARA-method second brain template (285 stars).

## CSS Snippet Collections

- [**Dmytro-Shulha snippets**](https://github.com/Dmytro-Shulha/obsidian-css-snippets) — Most common appearance fixes: scrollbars, headings, embeds, tables.
- [**r-u-s-h-i-k-e-s-h snippets**](https://github.com/r-u-s-h-i-k-e-s-h/Obsidian-CSS-Snippets) — Card layouts, callout styling, colored tags, folder decorations.
- [**deathau snippets**](https://github.com/deathau/obsidian-snippets) — Clutter-free headings, custom checkboxes, focused writing tweaks.
- [**Colored Sidebar**](https://github.com/CyanVoxel/Obsidian-Colored-Sidebar) — Assign distinct colors to top-level folders for fast visual navigation.

## Personal Published Vaults

Vaults the author publishes via Obsidian Publish or Quartz as a digital garden. Require per-vault verification before listing.

## Topic-Specific Reference Vaults

Vaults built around a single domain (history, philosophy, programming, TTRPG worldbuilding, etc.). Require per-vault verification before listing.

## Submit a Vault

If you maintain a public starter vault, template pack, or reference vault that other Obsidian users could fork or learn from, open a PR. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](../CONTRIBUTING.md). The vault must be publicly accessible (GitHub repo or Obsidian Publish URL) and actively maintained.
