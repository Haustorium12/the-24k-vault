# YouTube Directory

> YouTube channels covering Obsidian, PKM, and adjacent workflows. The README's [Resources section](../README.md#youtube-creators) lists curated picks; this is the broader index.
>
> Last reviewed: 2026-04-25.

## Comprehensive / General Obsidian

- [**Obsidian (official)**](https://www.youtube.com/@obsdmd) — Official Obsidian channel: release announcements, plugin showcases, behind-the-scenes.
- [**Linking Your Thinking (Nick Milo)**](https://www.youtube.com/@linkingyourthinking) — LYT framework, MOCs, Obsidian Flight School, deep PKM methodology.
- [**Mike Schmitz**](https://www.youtube.com/mikeschmitz) — Values-based productivity systems with Obsidian as the operating layer.

## Workflow & Power User

- [**Bryan Jenks**](https://www.youtube.com/@BryanJenks) — Zettelkasten in Obsidian, code, data, research workflows. Open source software focus.
- [**Curtis McHale**](https://www.youtube.com/c/CurtisMcHaleG) — iPad / macOS productivity with Obsidian for writing and book notes. Active blogger at [curtismchale.ca](https://curtismchale.ca).

## Adjacent (PKM, Methodology)

- [**The Obsidian Observer (Medium)**](https://medium.com/obsidian-observer) — Publication on Obsidian ideas and methodology with companion video content.
